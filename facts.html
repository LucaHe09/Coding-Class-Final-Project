Fun Facts About Miami

- It was the first U.S. city founded by a women
- 24.2 million people travelled to Miami in 2019
- Miami is the warmest U.S. city in the winter
- There are about 80 shipwrecks around Miami
- Miami is the only U.S. city by two national parks
- Most of Miami's beaches are man made, and was once a mangrove swamp
